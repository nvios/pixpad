Here are some useful tips to get you started:

  • Select the size of your canvas by changing the height and width parameters and then click on create
  • Choose a color clicking on the color button
  • Change the size of the brush selecting between S, M and L
  • Adjust the size of the grid using the "-" and "+" buttons to zoom in and out
  • Once you are ready, press done and you will be able to see the preview and instructions for your masterpiece
